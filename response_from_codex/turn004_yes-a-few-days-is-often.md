Yes, a few days is often enough. Try this routine:

1. Day 1–2:

    Start by softening the wax. Use 3–4 drops of an earwax softener (like Debrox or a few drops of mineral oil, baby oil, or diluted hydrogen peroxide) once or twice a day.
2. Day 3–4:

    Gently flush with warm water using a bulb syringe if your doctor approves.
